Diabetes: Safe Ranges & Diagnosis

Hemoglobin A1C

Normal: < 5.7% · Prediabetes: 5.7%–6.4% · Diabetes: ≥ 6.5%
Average sugar of last 2–3 months.

Fasting Plasma Glucose

Normal: < 100 mg/dL (5.6 mmol/L) · Prediabetes: 100–125 mg/dL (5.6–6.9 mmol/L) · Diabetes: ≥126 mg/dL (≥7.0 mmol/L)

2-hour OGTT

Normal: < 140 mg/dL (7.8 mmol/L) · Prediabetes: 140–199 mg/dL (7.8–11.0 mmol/L) · Diabetes: ≥200 mg/dL (≥11.1 mmol/L)

Random Glucose

Diabetes: ≥200 mg/dL (≥11.1 mmol/L) + symptoms
Who is diabetic?
A person is considered diabetic if they have:
- HbA1c ≥ 6.5%, OR
- Fasting ≥126 mg/dL, OR
- 2-hour OGTT ≥200 mg/dL, OR
- Random ≥200 mg/dL with symptoms.
(Usually confirmed with repeat testing.)
